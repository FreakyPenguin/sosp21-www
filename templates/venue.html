Koblenz, Germany

The City

Koblenz is a small city located at the confluence of two rivers - Rhine and Moselle. Originally established as a Roman military post by Drusus around 8 B.C., the city boasts more than 2000 years or rich history.

Getting to Koblenz

Koblenz is located nearly half way between Frankfurt and Cologne. Frankfurt airport is a major European hub with direct connections to most major international airports around the world. From Frankfurt airport, Koblenz is a 1h15m scenic train ride along the river Rhine. The train ride goes through the Upper Middle Rhine Valley which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
